NAU 27R Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [10 June 2024]

1. Information We Collect

We "Navodayans Uplift Association 27R" only collect the following information from users:

  • Sign-in Information: We collect your email address when you sign in to our services.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Contact Information: Used for payment processing and communication within the association.
  • Service Improvement: To improve our services and provide a better user experience.

3. Storage and Security

Data Storage: All user information is stored in Firestore located on servers in India.

Data Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Information Sharing

Internal Use: Your personal information is used solely within the association and is not shared with any third parties.

Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or to comply with legal processes.

5. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the information we hold about you by contacting our Head directly at jnvr27charityfund@gmail.com. Please include your email address and clearly state your request for access in the email.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information by contacting our Head directly at jnvr27charityfund@gmail.com. Please include your email address, the information you believe is inaccurate, and the correction you request in the email.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your information from our records by contacting our Head directly at jnvr27charityfund@gmail.com. Please include your email address and clearly state your request for deletion in the email. We will retain user sign-in data for five (5) years of inactivity, after which it will be deleted unless required for legal purposes.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website and will become effective immediately upon posting.
